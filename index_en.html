Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What should I do if the door code doesn’t work?

If you don’t have your key with you, call the emergency number listed in the footer.

Is smoking allowed on the property?

Smoking is not allowed inside the apartments. You can smoke only on balconies or in gardens. An ashtray is in the bathroom drawer.

Where are the trash bins?

You’ll find them near the entrance to the underground parking. You can access them through the garage or from the courtyard.

Where is the nearest shop?

Delikatesy Skorpion (Rozewska 1) is a small but well-stocked shop just around the corner, also open on Sundays.

Where is the nearest pharmacy?

Apteka Bursztynowa is located at Królewska 9, about 300 meters from Apartamenty Pilice.

Where can I find a taxi stand?

The taxi stand is right next to Hotel Ara. Off-season, we recommend ordering taxis from Władysławowo.
