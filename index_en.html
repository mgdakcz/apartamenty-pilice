Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What to do if my door code doesn't work?

If you don't have a key with you, call us at the number shown in the footer of the page.

Is smoking allowed on the premises?

Smoking is strictly prohibited in all apartments. Smoking is only allowed on balconies and in gardens. An ashtray can be found in the bathroom drawer.

Where are the trash bins located?

The bins are located at the entrance to the underground parking. Access is through the underground parking or from the courtyard.

Where is the nearest shop?

Delikatesy Skorpion (Rozewska 1) is a small but well-equipped shop just around the corner, also open on Sundays. How to get there?

Where is the nearest grocery market?

The closest is Polo Market, 160m from Pilice. How to get there?
In Mieroszyno, 4km from Pilice, there is a Biedronka. How to get there?

Where is the nearest petrol station?

The closest is in Łebcz, local station Renusz, 8 km. How to get there?
Further away, in Władysławowo, there is Orlen and CircleK, 10 km.
Orlen-how to get there? CircleK-how to get there?

Where is the nearest pharmacy?

Apteka Bursztynowa is located at 9 Królewska Street, about 300 meters from Apartamenty Pilice. How to get there?

Where can I find the nearest taxi stand?

The taxi stand is right next to Hotel Ara. Outside the high season, we recommend ordering taxis from Władysławowo. How to get there?

Where is the nearest bus stop?

The nearest stop is very close, only 190 meters from Pilice. How to get there?.
You can reach Gdynia and Karwia with PKS bus line 650. For Puck and Władysławowo, take line 668. Check the schedule here.

Where is the nearest hospital?

Municipal hospital in Puck, 18 km. How to get there?
Specialist hospital in Wejherowo, 38 km. How to get there?
